About Pcproton

Pcproton was created with one clear purpose — to build reliable and affordable PCs that deliver strong performance without forcing you to spend unnecessary money.

Why We’re Different

Many PC building companies prioritise fast sales, overpriced components, and upselling parts you don’t actually need. Once the purchase is complete, their customer service disappears — leaving customers with no support, no guidance, and no real care.

We take pride in doing things properly. At Pcproton, customer support is at the heart of everything we do. Whether you’re choosing parts, fixing an issue, or simply need honest advice, we’re here to help every step of the way.

Our Mission

We specialise in budget-focused builds that still deliver excellent performance. Our goal is to make powerful PCs accessible to everyone — students, gamers, creators, and anyone who needs strong performance on a tight budget.
